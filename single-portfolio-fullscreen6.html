Marketing Analytics

Project Scope

Paper Decorum was in the process of converting their brick and mortar business to an online model. In terms of past online advertisements, they had conducted email marketing using a mailing list of their existing clients but experienced relatively low conversion rates. As such, Paper Decorum wanted to optimize the use of social media platforms and enhance their utilization of analytical tools, such as Google Analytics, to tailor their marketing approach.

The analyst team analyzed Paper Decorum’s existing marketing strategy to identify the strengths within their mailing list and social media advertisements. They also enhanced the company's use of Google Analytics to better understand metrics such as conversion rates, regarding their marketing approach.


Project Objectives

• Analyze marketing program structure and existing conversion tracking methods

• Implement the use of analytical tools, such as Google Analytics to derive insights

• Adjust marketing campaign to ensure client retention, and drive growth
