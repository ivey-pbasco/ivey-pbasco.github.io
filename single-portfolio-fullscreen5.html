Ivey’s EDI Student Sentiment Analysis

Project Scope

The MSc Student Association (MSCA) needed insights on how to enhance the equity, diversity and inclusion (EDI) of marginalized groups within the Ivey community. As a response to the global outcry for social justice, the MSCA and program services team became interested in better understanding the common issues faced by marginalized groups within Ivey. These issues, have been publicly shared on social media accounts, including a student led Instagram page, Ivey at the Margins. In order to derive themes and overall student sentiment, a thorough analysis of the textual data, collected by the MSCA from the Ivey at the Margins page, was conducted.

The analyst team determined the frequencies of specific EDI complaints recorded on the Ivey at the Margins page as well as other relevant pages. With the information compiled, MSCA was able to identify issues reported in highest frequencies among students and develop a data-driven and systematic approach to improve the experiences of marginalized students within the greater Ivey community.


Project Objectives

• Identify common themes and student sentiment from social media posts

• Develop an action plan informed by data analysis to enhance Ivey’s EDI initiative


Project Analyst Team

Boyang (Mike) Fan; Paul Jin; Vu Nguyen

"The 'Ivey at the margins' Instagram page provided a platform for students to tell stories about their EDI experiences. Through analyzing these posts in this page using Python and Tableau, we uncovered the most pressing EDI issues faced by students. The highlight of the project is the Tableau dashboard our team designed for MScA, which shows the visualization of the summary statistics for the posts. The user could easily navigate the dashboard to analyze the posts by topic, location, event, year, main characters and their relationships. It will help the audience gain a deep understanding about the students’ EDI experiences and current issues they are facing at a high hierarchy."​


Project Partner Review

"This past term, Ivey Business School desired to renew and invigorate its focus on the core values of Equity, Diversity and Inclusion.  A key component of this process was trying to understand the issues our community faced and the opportunities that might exist.  The MSCA and MSc Program Services Team reached out to our newest Ivey MSc club, the Pro bono Analytics Club (PBAC). Given their collective strength in harnessing, analyzing, and interpreting large pools of unstructured data, we asked if they could explore the Ivey social media to see what insights it held. The net result was a robust, rich, and well-structured analysis of EDI at Ivey, developed and framed using data analytics best practice and tools.  There work and insights have been invaluable in guiding the MSc Program office and our partners towards the goal of defining our priorities for enhancing EDI at Ivey. Hats off to excellent work by the team of Vu, Paul, and Mike!"
