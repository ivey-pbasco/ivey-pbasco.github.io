FAM Coaching & Consulting - Client Marketing and Data Collection

Project Scope

This project aimed to provide FAM Coaching & Consulting with actionable insights on how to enhance their online exposure and client data collection strategy. FAM Coaching & Consulting is a startup organization that assists businesses with their equity, diversity and inclusion practices. As such, they were in the process of developing their marketing and data collection strategies.

The analyst team establish a data collection strategy to capture information by establishing traceable links between the company's current digital channels and website (i.e. Instagram and Facebook). In compiling this information, FAM CC was able to consistently evaluate the effectiveness of their marketing and further improve audience exposure to their services.


Project Objectives

• Strategize on how to enhance online exposure to a target market

• Establish connectivity across digital platforms to track website traffic

• Develop infrastructure to store client information and feedback
