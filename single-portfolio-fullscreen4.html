Data Insights and Unique User Identification Project

Project Scope

This project derived actionable insights from LCCLC’s reporting and data management structure. LCCLC previously used Microsoft Dynamics as their customer relationship and human resource management tool. However, they were uncertain of how to best analyze client data files prior to providing this information to the government. They needed insights to track metrics such as unique clients served, and services provided per client.

The analyst team got a good understanding of LCCLC’s data management structure, including their CRM and HRM software in order to improve reporting methods and identify areas of improvement within their organization so that they can better serve their community


Project Objectives

• Understand LCCLC’s current data management and reporting structure

• Develop an analytical process to derive insights from their data

• Provide actionable recommendations based on LCCLC’s data
