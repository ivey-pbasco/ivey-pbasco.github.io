Business Process Automation

Project Scope

South London Neighbourhood Resource Centre is a community space that offers programs and services including, but not limited to, Newcomer Settlement Services, Basic Needs and Support, Youth Programs, and Family and Parent Programs.

The team analyzed the current programming and evaluations services and enhanced the data collection process for the services provided.


Project Objectives

• Leverage the capabilities of power automate in developing a program participant pairing procedure

• Establish a data dictionary of feature descriptions and key tracking metrics within the application

Project Analyst Team
